Welcome to Kay Park Memorials

We are an independent, family-run business located on Grassyards Road in Kilmarnock. Coming from a family of stonemasons, spanning four generations, John Bayne started Kay Park Memorials as a continuation of his family business - previously Thomas Bayne and Sons. John has over thirty years of experience, providing quality headstones and cemetery services. We are RQMF (Register Qualification of Memorial Fitters) registered and pride ourselves in delivering a high standard of workmanship and unrivalled customer care. Although based in Kilmarnock, at Kay Park Memorials we expand our approachable, caring and professional services to the surrounding towns across Ayrshire and further afield - and even have examples of our work in the Isles of Skye, Arran and Gigha.

Although based in Kilmarnock, Kay Park Memorials also endeavour to offer a friendly, caring and professional service outwith this area, and indeed have had customers as far afield as Canada and South Africa. So if you're from Kilmarnock, Irvine, Ayr, anywhere in Ayrshire or further afield, feel free to contact us and we will be happy to assist you.

New Memorials

Visit our premises in Kilmarnock for a viewing of our memorial selection - where we can offer assistance or advice on the stone that's right for you and your loved one. Additionally, we have brochures for further options.

Memorial renovation/Additional Lettering

If additional lettering is required on an existing memorial, or renovation work is needed, we can provide assessments, advice on proceedings, and pricing estimates prior to commencing any work.

Designs and Types of Lettering

We have an expansive range of designs and letter types, with options of both modern and traditional methods of application. Our letters are gilded with twenty-three-point-five carat gold leaf, unless painted lettering is preferred. Leaded lettering is also available on request.

More Information

Here at Kay Park Memorial we have options of traditional, modern and bespoke headstones, with additional memorial services such as added lettering or renovations to existing headstones.

If searching for a new memorial, additional inscription, or renovation of an existing headstone, please feel free to visit our premises to view our selection or discuss your requirements. We are experienced and happy to provide support through the process of selecting the special memorial for your loved one. Alternatively, you can contact us to request a brochure, which we can email or post to your address. Furthermore, additional and unique memorial designs are available at your request.
